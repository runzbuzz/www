About

Meet Ahmad Alobaid

Founder & AI R&D Consultant

Runzbuzz is led by Ahmad Alobaid, an AI researcher and software engineer with over 13 years of experience helping startups and organizations build AI systems, conduct applied research, and develop production-ready software.

Ahmad studied Computer Science at Kuwait University before earning a Master's and a PhD in Artificial Intelligence from Universidad Politécnica de Madrid. He later completed a Master's in Statistics for Data Science at Universidad Carlos III de Madrid.

His experience spans telecom, academic research, EU-funded innovation projects, AI security research at NeuralTrust, and collaborations with startups across Spain and the UK.

Today, through Runzbuzz, Ahmad works directly with founders and teams to turn ambitious ideas into innovative AI products through strategy, applied AI research, and hands-on software development.
